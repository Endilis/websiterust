Get started in 2 minutes

Simple step-by-step setup — from adding the bot to your first notifications.

1

Add the bot to Discord

Click "Add bot", select your Discord server and grant permissions. The bot will create channels automatically.

2

Connect your Rust+ server

Open Rust+ on your phone, get a pairing code and enter it with !pair [code] in Discord.

3

Configure notifications

Choose what to track: raids, online status, Smart Switch. Use !settings to set up channels.

4

You're all set!
